About me

Hi! My name is Rijk Mulder, I'm 17 years old and come from the Netherlands, currently I am studying game development at GLU (Grafisch Lyceum Utrecht.) I am skilled in unity C# and have basic knowledge in UE5 with blueprints. I want to learn more about UE5 and learn C++. Most projects I have worked on so far I have worked on the player controller, because of that I know a lot about making player controllers and how to make a game satisfying, simply by having good player movement. When I am not working on a game I enjoy playing games myself (duh), go to the gym and watch Formula 1.

Scrapper.

Scrapper was the last project I made in my first year at GLU. It is a Diablo inspired twinstick shooter, though it never reached its full potential I am still proud of what i contributed in this project.
