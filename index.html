About me

Hi! My name is Rijk Mulder, I'm 17 years old and come from the Netherlands, currently I am studying game development at GLU (Grafisch Lyceum Utrecht.) I am skilled in unity C# and have basic knowledge in UE5 with blueprints. Most projects I have worked on so far I have worked on the player controller, because of that I know a lot about making player controllers and how to make a game satisfying, simply by having good player movement.

When I am not working on a game I enjoy playing games myself (no surprises there), go to the gym and watch Formula 1.
